Pong

An introduction to HTML5 canvas by making a single-player pong game.

About

I realise it's not fun at all (at least not to me), but it has moving objects, basic collision detection, and is a simple game that helped me get a sense of game developing in javascript.

Feel free to look at the code. Questions and suggestions are both welcome. I'm not new to game developing, but I am to web stuff, so I may be using CSS/js/HTML inappropriately or inefficiently.
